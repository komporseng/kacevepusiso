Laura Sanko's journey to becoming a UFC commentator has been marked by significant milestones and challenges.

From her background as a professional MMA fighter to her transition into broadcasting and commentary, Sanko's career trajectory reflects determination, resilience, and a commitment to excellence. 

Laura Sanko's Background

Laura Sanko, born on December 7, 1982, in Chicago, Illinois, is an American mixed martial arts commentator.

She attended William Jewell College and later ventured into the world of mixed martial arts.

Notably, Sanko adopted the nickname "Fancy" during her brief fighting career after an acquaintance remarked that she was too fancy to fight, rather than just train in mixed martial arts.

Transition from Fighter to Broadcaster

Sanko's professional MMA record includes one match and one win, with a victory via submission (rear-naked choke) at Invicta FC 4 in 2013. But she halted her fighting career shortly after this match due to family reasons.

Sanko later returned to the MMA scene, not as a fighter, but as a commentator and interviewer. Her transition from fighter to broadcaster showcases her adaptability and passion for the sport.

Breaking Barriers in Broadcasting

In 2021, Laura Sanko made history as the first female commentator to work on a broadcast in the UFC's Zuffa or Endeavor era, providing color commentary for Dana White's Contender Series.

This achievement exemplifies her dedication and expertise in the field of MMA commentary.

In January 2023, Sanko became the first female color commentator to appear on a UFC broadcast since Kathy Long at UFC 1 in 1993, marking a significant milestone in her career.

Challenges Faced and Misconceptions Addressed

Despite her accomplishments, Sanko has had to contend with baseless rumors and unfair criticisms throughout her career.

In 2022, she addressed critics who claimed that she only secured her job due to her looks, emphasizing that her male co-workers' respect for her professional abilities has played a crucial role in her success.

Sanko has highlighted the challenges faced by women in the industry, shedding light on the unfair comments and rumors that often plague their careers.

Laura Sanko's journey to becoming a UFC commentator has been a testament to her resilience and determination.

From her early days as a professional MMA fighter to her groundbreaking achievements as a commentator, Sanko's career trajectory exemplifies her passion for the sport and her unwavering commitment to excellence.
