Who Is Sergio Steele? His Bio, Age, Wife, Height, Family

May 2024 · 4 minute read

Having an attitude in a cooking show is a big NoNo. But Sergio Steele got a second chance despite not listening to the Next Level Chef judges and doing a shockingly poor job on his salmon dish.

So, who is he? Keep reading this Sergio Steele Bio to learn all about him.

Sergio Steele On Next Level Chef

Gordan Ramsey’s new cooking show Next Level Chef premiered on Jan 2, 2022, and it featured Sergio Steele alongside 14 other contestants. Just like the title suggests NLC took three teams, who competed on three levels of a massive three-story kitchen for a prize of a quarter of a million dollars.

The top-level which included the best line gadget and gizmo a cook could ever wish for was mentored by judge Nyesha Arrington. The middle level, with an essentially equipped standard kitchen, was handed by Richard Blais. And the Basement Level, which only received the leftovers, was undoubtedly chef Gordon Ramsay’s.

As seen on the first ep, the 15 contestants were split into groups of five and sent to different levels of the kitchen. So, after each of them cooked and presented their dishes, the selection began. Well, Sergio who poorly cooked a salmon on the top floor! and Courtney, a personal chef cooking in The Basement, were the final two left to be picked.

And to everyone’s surprise, chef Nyesha decided to give Sergio a second chance and took him into her team.

Given his arrogance and vanity, many were certain that he would be the next to go home.

Joining Sergio in this inaugural season were chefs —  Zachary Adams, Reuel Vincent, Amber Rebold, Mariah Scott, Jonathan Harrison, Kenny Everett, Ae Southammavong, Angie Ragan, Courtney Brown, Tricia Wang, Roice Bethel, Gary Marandola, Stephanie “Pyet” DeSpain, and Devonnie Black.

Recalling his time on the show, Sergio shared that it was an “honestly unreal experience.” Also, he explained himself to a “beast in the kitchen that can keep a two-man station running by (himself) at 7:30 no problem.” Guess that explains his arrogancy.

Sergio Steele Wife

Sergio Steele Career

Sergio Steele wasn’t only a chef who owned the meal prep company Steel Meal but also the “Regional Sales Manager” of Dynamik Muscle and Prime Nutrition. Furthermore, he also worked as an “Inside Sales Representative” at Braman Motorcars as of 2022.

Before that, Sergio worked as the “Regional Sales Manager” at REDCON1 and a “Wholesale Account Executive” at Blackstone Labs.

This professional chef’s journey in the restaurant industry began at the mere age of 14 with a job as a dishwasher. But just within 3 months, he transitioned to a role as a cook and began working under renowned chefs in Palm Beach. By 18, he became a well-known chef himself.

So, after his success in fine dining, Sergio then turned into the supplement industry. He trained alongside the best bodybuilders and also earned a personal training certification during this time.

Today, Sergio boats two careers and is pretty popular on both.
