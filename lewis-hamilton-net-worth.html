Lewis Hamilton age, birthplace and parents

He was born on January 7, 1985, in Stevenage (England) to father Anthony Hamilton and Carmen Larbalestier (mother).

Lewis Hamilton education

This prolific man had his education at the Jonh Henry Newman School where he developed the skills and passion for racing specifically in 1993.

How did Lewis Hamilton become famous?

He officially began his car racing career in 2001, with the British Renault Winter series finishing 5th in the standings. He made his debut with Manor in the 2004 Formula series, finishing fifth and in the championship.

Hamilton’s successful and iconic winning races happened when he signed with McLaren in 2007. This beautiful journey ended in 2012 after gaining legendary status.

He later joined Mercedes in 2013 and later this year became the first driver to reach 100 pole positions in Formula One History.

Lewis Hamilton height: How tall is Lewis Hamilton?

He is 5.7 feet (1.74 meters) tall.

Lewis Hamilton wife, relationships, girlfriends

He is currently single per official sources but has been linked to romantic relationships with Danielle Lloyd in 2002, Jodia Ma 2003-2007, Vivian Burkhardt in 2008.

He was later with American singer Nicole Scherzinger from later parts of 2008 to 2015

He had the opportunity to date Rihanna, Rita Ora and Nicki Minaj in the years 2015,2016 and 2018, respectively.

Lewis Hamilton net worth

Lewis Hamilton salary

Lewis Hamilton cars

He owns a Ferrari LaFerrari, a Pagani Zonda 760 LH, which was made specifically for him, a Mclaren P1,1966 Shelby 427 Cobra, Mercedes-AMG Project One and an MV Agusta Lewis Hamilton Edition 8000RR which are all part of his insatiable car collections.
