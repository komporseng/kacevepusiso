Southern Charm Star Madison LeCroy Got Married To Brett Randle

July 2024 · 3 minute read

Madison LeCroy from Southern Charm is a married lady. On Saturday, November 19, the reality personality married Brett Randle in a lovely ceremony in Mexico. The couple exchanged vows in front of close relatives and friends at the Chablé Maroma hotel in Riviera Maya. Hudson, the Southern Charm star’s 10-year-old son, was the ring bearer during the wedding. The pair apparently said “I do” on November 16 in a courtroom ceremony in Charleston, South Carolina. After a short courtship, the pair got engaged last year.

Madison LeCroy of Southern Charm got engaged to Brett Randle in 2021.

Madison LeCroy of Southern Charm married her fiancé Brett Randle after they got engaged in October of last year. After dating for seven months, the former basketball star proposed on October 8, 2021. Madison confirmed her engagement to Us at the time, saying:

“I’m thrilled to report that I’ve gotten engaged!” Oh, my goodness. I’m really happy, and I feel like I’ve been trying to keep it in for a long time, and it’s been one of the most difficult things I’ve had to do.”

Brett proposed to her and her kid during a delayed birthday party, she told People. She stated:

“I was honestly not anticipating that day.” We traveled a lot this summer, so I assumed it would come around that time.”

She continued, saying:

“Then we get back home, and I’m thinking it’s just a birthday dinner, and my son’s over here playing around, and I turn around, and my fiancé is down on one knee and proposing to me in my living room.” It was very personal. I’ve been weeping uncontrollably from delight. [I’m] overjoyed.”

Brett made certain to get Hudson’s approval before making the proposal. Hudson’s sole need was that he be a “part of the proposal.” Madison remembered:

“‘My mom, I know she’d adore a limo,’ Hudson says. So let’s get a limo and take her out to supper to celebrate her belated birthday.”

In April 2021, Madison was attending a friend’s bachelorette party in Scottsdale, Arizona, when they met. Madison made the romance public on Instagram in June. Until then, she had kept Brett’s identity hidden and had only disclosed his first name. However, since Brett lived in California and Madison and her kid live in South Carolina, their relationship was largely long-distance. However, in May, the couple purchased their first house together.

Madison LeCroy of Southern Charm made the wedding planning “stress-free.”

Madison of Southern Charm made her wedding preparation “stress-free.”” She hired a professional wedding planner and limited the guest list to a minimum. She talked to E! News about her private wedding ceremony:

“I’m only expecting 30 people. It’s a destination, and my fiancé is one of eight children, so it’s mostly his and my family’s. Very personal.”

Adding:

“It makes me really happy. “All I’m going to do is show up and marry the guy of my dreams.”

She was formerly married to Josh Hughes, with whom she had a son named Hudson. The couple divorced in 2015. In the past, the reality star has been associated to Austen Kroll, Jay Cutler, and John Pringle.
