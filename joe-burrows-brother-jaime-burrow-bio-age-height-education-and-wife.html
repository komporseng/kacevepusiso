Joe Burrows brother Jaime Burrow, Bio, Age, Height, Education, and wife.

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Jaime Burrow (Age: 43 years) was born in 1978 in Ames, Iowa, USA. He is the son of Jim Burrow. He has two younger brothers Dan and Joe Burrow. Jaime attended Ames High School in Ames, Iowa. After that, he enrolled at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln where he played college football for four years on the University football team. Jaime graduated from the University of Nebraska in 2001.

Jaime Burrow is a former American footballer. He is the elder brother of Dan and Joe Burrow. Dan also played American football at the University of Nebraska football team. His little brother Joe is an American footballer for the Cincinnati Bengals of the National Football League. His father Jim Burrow is a former American footballer and football coach.

Jaime is a former American football player from the University of Nebraska. He played for four years as a middle linebacker at The Nebraska Connercka football team from 1998 to 2001. He played a total of 47 games for the University football team. He earned 116 touchdowns in his college career.
