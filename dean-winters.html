While studying in London, he worked as a model to supplement his income. He returned to Manhattan after that and began working as a bartender in pubs and bars. He also attended the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art in London for a summer intensive. In the late 2000s, he became interested in pursuing an acting career and began appearing in theatre and auditioning for roles in films and television.
